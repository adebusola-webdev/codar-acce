What do you need to Apply?

  • Innovative and Scalable Idea: Startups with unique and innovative business ideas that have the potential for rapid growth and scalability.
  • Strong and Committed Team: Startup's team should ensure they have the right mix of skills, experience, and dedication needed to execute the business plan successfully.
  • Market Traction: Startups that have already gained some traction in the market, such as initial customers, users, or revenue, are more likely to be considered for acceleration.
  • Market Potential: The market potential and size of the startup's target market would be determined to know if there is a significant opportunity for growth.
  • Coachability: Startups that are open to feedback, willing to learn, and can demonstrate their ability to adapt and pivot based on market feedback would be highly regarded.
  • Clear Business Model: The startup should have a well-defined and viable business model that explains how it plans to generate revenue and sustain its growth.
  • Scalability and Growth Plan: Startups should have a clear plan for scaling their operations and expanding their market reach.
  • Time Commitment: Startups must be prepared to commit a significant amount of time and effort to the accelerator program, as it typically involves intensive mentoring and support
